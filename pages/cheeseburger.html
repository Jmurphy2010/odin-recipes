Recipe | Cheeseburger

This is a recipe for a cheeseburger!

  1. Cut onions, tomatoes, lettuce and get out your ketchup, mustard, mayo
  2. Shape your beef patties - slightly larger than your buns. No more than 3/4in thick
  3. Fire up the grill and get your patties staged for grilling
  4. Grill the beef patties, flipping as little as possible to preserve the juicy flavor
  5. Once patties are nearly done, put a slice of cheese on each patty and toast your burger buns. Close the lid to melt the cheese
  6. Remove from grill once both sides are a deep charred brown
  7. Put 1-2 patties on each set of buns. Top with condiments to hearts content
