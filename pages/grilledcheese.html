Recipe | Grilled Cheese

This is a recipe for a great grilled cheese!

Ingredients: White bread, cheese, mayo or butter, heat, a can-do attitude

  1. Generously butter (or mayo) both sides of two pieces of bread
  2. Put slices of cheese in between slices of bread
  3. Grill the cheese sandwich on a hot pan, flipping periodically
  4. Remove from pan once both sides are browned and cheese is gooey
