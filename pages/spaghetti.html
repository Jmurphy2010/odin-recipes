Recipe | Spaghetti

This is a recipe for tasty spaghetti!

  1. Dice onion and mince garlic, cry from cutting onion
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, add onion and garlic
  3. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, rind from parmesan wedge
  4. Once sauce is bubbling, lower heat to simmer, cover
  5. Boil water in a pot, add salt
  6. Add spaghetti noodles and cook til al dente
  7. Take 1/2 cup of pasta water and stir into sauce
  8. Strain spaghetti noodles and plate
  9. Immediately before eating, top noodles with sauce and freshly grated parmesan
